About the Artist

Melanie Adele Martinez is an American singer-songwriter, screenwriter, poet, director, actress, dancer, producer, and photographer.She is known for writing songs that discuss social issues.

Early Life

Melanie Adele Martinez, she first auditioned for the American television vocal talent show The Voice and became a member of Team Adam, where her popularity rapidly grew among fans. Within the fifth week, she was eliminated, which subsequently prompted her to work on original material.

Success

Melanie Martinez has created 3 new albums, 2 eps, and won Artist of the Year, - Alternative or Indie, including a competition, to best visual effects on her music video "VOID"
